Sample of Reputation Policy 
Generic:
trans(user1,user2,positiveTransactions,NegativeTransactions).

Transactions should be positive integers.

Example:

trans(tom, mike, 10, 5).
trans(tom, harold, 10, 5).
trans(mike, tom, 5, 10000).
trans(mike, harold, 1000, 5).
trans(harold, tom, 5, 10000).
trans(harold, tim, 100, 10).
trans(harold, mike, 100, 10).
trans(tim, tom, 9, 100).
Sample of Similarity Policy 
Generic:
sim(issuer,concept1,concept2,similarityValue). 

Similarity Value between 0 and 1

Example:

sim(red,neurology,psychiatry,0.5).
sim(blue,neurology,psychiatry,0.9).
sim(brown,neurology,psychiatry,0.7).
sim(black,neurology,psychiatry,0.6).
sim(red,thoracic_surgery,pulmonology,0.3).
sim(blue,thoracic_surgery,pulmonology,0.65).
sim(brown,thoracic_surgery,pulmonology,0.46).
sim(black,thoracic_surgery,pulmonology,0.69).
sim(black,neurology,pulmonology,0.9).
sim(brown,neurology,pulmonology,1.0).
sim(red,neurology,pulmonology,1.0).
sim(blue,neurology,pulmonology,1.0).
Credential Policy Sample (GEM Policies) 
Generic Facts:

cred(issuer,credential,entity).

Generic Rules:

cred(issuer,credential,entity) :- cred(issuer1,credential1,entity),cred(issuer2,credential2,entity1). 

Example:

cred(c2,member,X) :- cred(c1,member,X).
cred(c2,member,X) :- cred(ri,member,X). 
cred(c2,member,alice).
